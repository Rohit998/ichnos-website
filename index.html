﻿Employee Contact Tracing System

Enables contact tracing of your employees within your own premises by using customized QR Codes, which can be deployed quickly on the cloud and which comes with a temperature reading device.

Benefits of Employee Contact Tracing System!

  • Implement and deploy within 48 hours on the cloud.
  • Saves time as ECTS reduces manual data entry and recording of employee movement within your premise(s).
  • Temperature alerts – manual included – automated solution on request.
  • Business Intelligence (BI) Dashboard reporting for the employees movements.
  • Multi-layered contact tracing reports.
  • Ability to extract the employees data from HR system once integration is done.
  • Can be deployed and integrated remotely without the need to expose any personnel or disrupt company’s current operations.
  • Password protected to provide security of data.
  • Mobile enabled and instantaneous e-mail alerts.
